ABOUT US

Welcome to the Gujarat Security Operations Center (SOC), a leading destination for proactive cybersecurity measures and intelligence in the region. The SOC is dedicated unit under the aegis of Department of Science and Technology to safeguard the digital infrastructure of Government of Gujarat through advanced security measures, vigilant monitoring, and proactive threat detection.

Gujarat Security Operations Center (GSOC) is established in May 2022. GSOC is the state nodal agency for responding to computer security incidents for State Critical Information Infrastructure. GSOC has been designated to serve as the state agency to perform the following functions in the area of cyber security.

GSOC Functions

Identification Critical Information Infrastructure
Issue security guidelines, advisories
Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing
Cyber Security Awareness and Capacity Building
